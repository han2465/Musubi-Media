About

We build for the operators we work alongside.

Musubi Media was founded by Anthony Han — restaurant owner (Soul Chicken), technologist, and Clover payment partner based in Honolulu. After running Soul Chicken inside Midtown Eats food court and watching vendors struggle with tools not designed for multi-operator environments, we started building the platform we wished existed.

Our advantage is perspective: we're not a Silicon Valley software company selling to restaurants — we ARE the operator. Every feature is tested against the real constraints of a busy Hawaii kitchen, tight vendor margins, and the specific patterns of local dining culture.
