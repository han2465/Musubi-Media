About

We build for the operators we work alongside.

Musubi Media is a Honolulu-based technology company founded by Anthony Han — restaurant owner (Soul Chicken), Clover payment partner, and longtime software and hardware developer. What started as a karaoke app in 2018 has grown into a portfolio of platforms, apps, and services built for Hawaii's hospitality and retail operators.

Our advantage is perspective: we're not a Silicon Valley software company selling to local businesses — we ARE one. Every product is tested against the real constraints of a busy Hawaii kitchen, tight vendor margins, and the specific patterns of local culture.
